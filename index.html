Meet The Trainers

Aaron Stephens

Speed / Strength

Aaron is a long time veteran at Run Buddy. Originally winning gold in the Men's 100M Dash at the 2002 Olympics held in Salt Lake City, Utah, Aaron is your go to for running speed. We're so lucky to have Aaron working with us, and you will feel a true runner's spirit when wokring with him!

Joanna Gill

Endurance

Joanna is a regular marathon runner with over 15+ marathons to her name. Originally from Boston, MA, Joanna moved to Utah in order to train for marathons at a higher elevation. In her own words: "I've completed The Boston Marathon year after year and have found myself plaving within the top 20 runners each time. I'm very excited about training in the mountains here in Utah."
